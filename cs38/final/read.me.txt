You can walk around the shop and inspect - 'i' the items,
you can pick up items - 'p' and theyll be added to your inventory.
You can buy items as long as you have enough gold. I didn't get a chance
to implement selling or randomized room / shop generation. This was alot
more difficult than I anticipated. But lots of fun!

The "game" as it is just attempts to mimic Nethack (http://nethack.org/)
and other turn based text adventures. I picked the store element because it
seemed like a good constraint to work in for my first attempt at 
something like this.
